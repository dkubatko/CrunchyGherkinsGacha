Generate a 5:7 detailed collectible trading card using the provided
template image as layout reference, and the character image as the
subject of the illustration.

## Goals

1. The card must be a high-quality character portrait of "{name}".
2. The person or character depicted on the card must remain clearly
   and unmistakably recognizable from the input photo.

## Art Style & Theme

- High-quality, detailed digital illustration.
- There is NO thematic modification — this is a clean, unmodified
  base character card.
- The background should be a simple, elegant setting that complements
  the character without overwhelming them.

## Card Layout

- **Main artwork:** The main area of the template should contain the
  character and a clean, complementary background. This artwork must
  fill the entire area within the border of the card.
- **Nameplate:** The styled nameplate must be a standalone, opaque
  "floating" piece layered on top of the main artwork, disconnected
  from the border of the card.
- **Final image:** Must retain the exact edge-to-edge dimensions of
  the provided template, with no added external padding, margins,
  or borders.

## Character Likeness

- The character's unique identifying facial features and overall
  appearance must remain recognizable.
- Stylize and render the character in a polished digital illustration
  style. Do NOT reuse or lightly filter the original photo.
- Push the artistic interpretation to its most visually striking form
  while ensuring the image is successfully generated.

## Border

- The border's outer edge must be the image's edge — no gap, margin,
  or artwork between the border and the image boundary. The border
  must NOT float inset within the card interior.
- Elegant, understated styling — clean and refined without heavy
  thematic elements.
- No more than 10% of total card area.

## Nameplate

- Displays only the character name "{name}".
- Clean, elegant, and readable.
- No more than 15% of total card area.
- Horizontally centered, fitting in one line.
- No other text anywhere on the card.

## Rarity Color

The card's border and nameplate must use a {color} color palette
to indicate card rarity. This color applies only to the border and
the nameplate. The main artwork and character use any colors that
best represent the subject.

## Creativity Factor: {creativeness_factor}/100

Controls the visual complexity of border and nameplate styling.
Creativity factor does NOT control the art style genre or the degree
of deviation from the character's likeness.
- **Low (e.g. 10):** Simple, clean design with minor details and
  effects on the border and nameplate.
- **Medium (e.g. 50):** Refined details with balanced decorative
  elements and noticeable but not overwhelming styling.
- **High (e.g. 90):** Sophisticated, intricate design work with
  dynamic lighting, depth, and complex effects on border and
  nameplate.
