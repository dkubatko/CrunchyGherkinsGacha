## Unique Aspect Requirements

This is a "Unique" rarity aspect sphere — a one-of-a-kind creation
of the highest tier.

- The sphere design must be spectacular in quality, detail, and
  thematic execution.
- Push the boundaries of visual complexity: intricate interior scenes,
  mesmerizing lighting, extraordinary depth and detail.
- The sphere should feel like a legendary artifact — unmistakably
  premium.
- **Color freedom:** You are NOT bound by any rarity color scheme.
  Choose any color palette that best enhances the sphere's theme
  and visual impact.
