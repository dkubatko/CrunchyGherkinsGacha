Generate a completely fresh 5:7 collectible trading card featuring the
provided character in a world defined by their equipped aspects.

This is a full regeneration — create a brand new image from scratch
with a different composition, pose, background, and artistic
interpretation than any previous version.

## Input Images

1. **Card template** — rarity layout reference.
2. **Character photo** — the subject to depict on the card.
3. **Aspect reference images** — each shows an aspect's visual identity
   rendered inside a decorative glass orb.

Each reference orb shows what the aspect's theme represents. Use
each reference to understand its theme, then incorporate it into the
card's scene in a way that fits naturally. Themes can appear directly
(e.g., the character holding or wearing the item) or indirectly
(e.g., the setting, atmosphere, or context reflecting it) — whatever
produces the most cohesive result.
The glass orb is decorative packaging — it must never appear on
the card in any form.

## Card Details

- Card name: "{card_name}"
- Equipped aspects: {aspects}

## Creative Direction

Create a visual hybrid that blends the character with the fusion of
all equipped aspect themes. Each reference image shows what a theme
represents — incorporate all themes into a single cohesive setting.

The character should appear as a natural inhabitant of this themed
world: their clothing, pose, expression, and surroundings all shaped
by the combined themes. Push the thematic interpretation to its most
visually striking form while ensuring the image is successfully
generated.

Render the character as a high-quality digital illustration — stylize
and transform them to fit the fused theme. Do not reuse or lightly
filter the original photo. The character's unique identifying facial
features must remain recognizable.

## Card Layout

- Main artwork: character and themed background filling the entire
  area within the border.
- Nameplate: a standalone, opaque, floating piece layered on top of
  the artwork, displaying "{card_name}". Nameplate border, background,
  and text must be styled with design elements reflecting the card's
  theme. No more than 15% of total card area. Centered, one line.
- Border: styled with thematic patterns, textures, depth, and effects
  reflecting the combined aspect themes. The border's outer edge must
  be the image's edge — no gap or margin between the border and the
  image boundary. It must NOT float inset within the card. No more
  than 10% of total card area. Never leave a plain, solid color
  border.
- Final image must retain exact edge-to-edge dimensions of the
  template. No added padding.
- No text other than "{card_name}" on the nameplate.

## Rarity Color

Border and nameplate use a {color} color palette. Main artwork is
not constrained by this color.

## Creativity Factor: {creativeness_factor}/100

The creativity factor controls the visual complexity of the card's
design, including border, nameplate, and thematic integration. It
does NOT control the degree of deviation from the character's likeness.
- **Low (e.g. 10):** Simple, clean design with themes lightly applied.
  Minor details and effects on the border and nameplate.
- **Medium (e.g. 50):** Balanced thematic detail throughout. Complex
  patterns and decorative elements across the card's design.
- **High (e.g. 90):** Spectacular, intricate fusion of all aspects.
  Dynamic lighting, depth, and sophisticated thematic effects on
  every element.
